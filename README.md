# 📊 Superstore Sales Data Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview
This project presents a professional exploratory and business-focused analysis of a **Superstore Orders dataset** containing over **51,000+ transactions**. The dataset includes customer, product, shipping, and financial information across multiple markets and regions.

The objective of this project is to uncover insights related to **sales performance, profitability, customer segments, shipping efficiency, and product categories**, helping support data-driven business decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
The key goals of this analysis include:

- Analyze overall **sales and profit performance**
- Identify the **most profitable** and **least profitable** categories and sub-categories
- Evaluate the impact of **discounts** on profit
- Explore customer segments and their contribution to revenue
- Analyze market and regional performance
- Detect patterns behind **loss-making orders**
- Provide business recommendations based on findings

---

## 📂 Dataset Description
The dataset contains detailed transactional order data with features such as:

- Order details (Order ID, Order Date, Ship Date, Order Priority)
- Customer information (Customer ID, Segment)
- Geographic information (Country, State, City, Region, Market)
- Product details (Category, Sub-Category, Product Name)
- Business metrics (Sales, Profit, Discount, Shipping Cost, Quantity)

**Dataset Size**
- Rows: 51,305
- Columns: 30+ attributes

## 🔍 Key Analysis Areas

### 📈 Sales and Profit Trends
- Yearly and monthly sales performance
- Profit trends across time periods

### 🛒 Category and Product Insights
- Top-selling categories and sub-categories
- Products contributing the most to profit
- Products generating repeated losses

### 👥 Customer Segment Analysis
- Sales and profit contribution by customer segment
- Customer groups with highest business value

### 🌍 Market and Regional Performance
- Comparison of sales and profit across markets
- Identification of high-performing and low-performing regions

### 🚚 Shipping and Logistics Efficiency
- Shipping mode distribution
- Relationship between shipping cost and profitability

### ❌ Loss and Discount Analysis
- Orders with negative profit
- Discount levels and their effect on profit margins
